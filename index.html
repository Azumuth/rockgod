Rock God Design

The following online style guide is for Rock God Design, a boutique digital design and development company from Auckland, New Zealand. This guide highlights all written, visual, and graphic elements that encompass our brand. These standards are intended to be tools and guidelines that enable us to maintain a consistant voice to the public and provide you with the most effective campaign for using our brand. Please refer to this when developing any internal or external communications using the Rock Gods Design logo and brand.

Minimum logo size should be w .75” x h .27” or h .35” with the tag. Anything smaller will be illegible.
